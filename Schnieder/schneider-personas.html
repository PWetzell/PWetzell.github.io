Research output · User personas

Personas built from direct customer research.

Tomo and William were not defined upfront — they emerged from interviews and workflow observations with Schneider customers recruited through internal Schneider channels. Facilities managers and maintenance technicians at customer organizations, observed directly as they subscribed to new modules and managed existing ones.

T
Tomo
Facility Manager
Responsibilities
Command CenterStreamlines multiple use cases, technologies, and vendors into one hub with global scalability.
Building OperationsOversees daily, weekly and monthly facility capabilities and controls changing occupancy thresholds.
Asset TrackingUses IoT to allocate and track equipment and high-profile assets across floors and buildings.
3rd Party IntegrationsBuilds intelligent partner ecosystems for more holistic facilities management.
Goals
Reduce carbon footprint and energy savings.
Improve response time across facilities.
Compliance monitoring throughout facility.
System configuration of compliance monitoring.
Challenges
Improve readability of equipment data.
Wants a dashboard with real-time monitoring data.
Needs
Access to weather forecasts for energy management.
Comparison tools for reporting and compliance.
W
William
Maintenance Technician
Responsibilities
Routine MaintenancePerforms routine equipment maintenance on schedule.
Emergency ResponseResponds to equipment breakdowns and failures in real time.
Compliance RecordingRecords maintenance process and compliance requirements.
Monitoring ApplicationsLeverages the organization's maintenance and monitoring apps.
Goals
Keep equipment running smoothly through preventative maintenance.
Quicker triage time and greater accuracy.
Proactive maintenance schedule across all assets.
Challenges
Quick access to OEM information about parts and equipment.
Ability to adapt maintenance plan on the fly.
Ability to add notes and comments to assets.
Handle a variety of new and changing technology.
Needs
Able to do his job without additional difficulties.
Solvable problems with resources to fix them efficiently.
