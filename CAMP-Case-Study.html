The Problem

Aircraft maintenance technicians were submitting oil samples on paper — incomplete data, misidentified filters, duplicate submissions, and costly resubmissions.

Honeywell partnered with CAMP Systems to modernize the oil and filter sample submission process for aircraft maintenance teams. The legacy workflow relied on paper checklists, which led to frequent errors in sample identification, incorrect filter usage, wasted hours, and costly resubmissions.

The new mobile experience replaced manual entry with guided forms and validation logic — reducing errors, accelerating turnaround, and improving technician confidence in maintenance decisions.

The core tension

Aviation maintenance demands zero-error data — but the paper-based workflow made errors nearly inevitable.

In aircraft maintenance, a wrong filter selection or an incomplete sample submission isn't just inefficient — it can delay critical safety diagnostics. The paper process had no validation, no version control, and no real-time feedback. Technicians were working in the field without the system support to catch errors before they became costly resubmissions or compromised maintenance records.

My Role

Led UX design for the GoDirect SOAP mobile app — from workflow analysis and wireframes through interactive prototypes and final UI.

What I owned
End-to-end UX — workflow analysis with maintenance teams, wireframes, interactive prototypes, modular UI component design, contextual filter recommendation logic, lab proximity mapping, and final mobile UI screens.
The platform ecosystem
Designed for the CAMP Systems platform — the primary interface where operators view SOAP results, compliance, and engine trend reports. Integrates Honeywell's HEAT and HDA diagnostic toolkits, with Ensemble handling real-time data transmission and Forge IoT APM powering predictive engine health analytics.
The stakes
Aviation maintenance has zero tolerance for data errors. Every UI decision — validation logic, filter selection guidance, lab routing — had a direct downstream impact on sample accuracy, turnaround time, and technician safety confidence in the field.

Streamlining oil sampling and reducing errors — the GoDirect SOAP app shipped on Android.

The GoDirect SOAP mobile app reduced documentation errors, accelerated sample turnaround time, and improved technician confidence by delivering guided inputs, accurate filter-hour calculations, and real-time results that streamlined the entire oil analysis workflow.

Reduced Resubmissions

What I'd do differently

Get field technicians into usability testing earlier — and in the actual field environment. The workflow analysis was thorough, but testing prototypes in a conference room doesn't replicate the conditions under which this app actually gets used: on a tarmac, in bright sunlight, with gloves on, under time pressure. The final UI made good decisions about tap target size and contrast, but some of those decisions came from iteration feedback rather than upfront testing in context. For a mobile app in a safety-critical environment, environmental testing should be a first-class part of the research plan from day one.
